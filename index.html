Noah Corbett

Doctoral Candidate

Florida Atlantic University
Department of Mathematics and Statistics

    Hi there! I'm a fourth-year doctoral candidate studying computational dynamical systems. My advisors are Jason Mireles-James and Vincent Naudot.
    My reserach focuses primarily on using techniques from functional analysis to calculate and validate connecting orbits in chaotic dynamical systems. In particular, I employ computer-assited proofs to rigorously study nonlinear ordinary differential equations, travelling wave solutions of partial differential equations, and state-dependent delay differential equations.


American Mathematical Society

    Since Fall 2023, I have been serving as President of the FAU AMS Student Chapter. With the help of the AMS officers and other volunteers from the department, we have been able to plan many exciting activities for the department. Our biggest and most recent event of the year, in collaboration with the AWM and SIAM student chapters, was the 2024 Florida Women in Math Day.
